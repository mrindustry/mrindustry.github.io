“Mr. Industry add a shot of menace for every drop of joy, a savage piece of noise to match each moment of jazzy musicality. It’s a jagged assault until it’s not, and it weaves in between those two poles throughout, via a truly enormous and inescapable sound. Magnificent, threatening, and utterly wild” - The Underground Stage


Carved from the Australian post-punk landscape yet standing on entirely new terrain, Mr. Industry are paving the future of guitar-based music in Australia, dancing solemnly and explosively between alt-indie, post-hardcore and post-punk. As sincere as they are melodically captivating, Mr. Industry have made waves nationally with their dynamic and emotional live performance since forming in Canberra in 2021. Now based in Melbourne, the quintet are gearing up to launch their debut EP, "Dancing To My Own Internal Rhythm" in 2025.


Mr. Industry have supported Japan's Haru Nemuri on tour and performed on stage alongside countless Australian acts including Radio Free Alice, The Belair Lip Bombs, These New South Whales, Mac The Knife, Sonic Reducer, Sesame Girl and Garage Sale.
